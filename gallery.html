ADOBE IMAGE PROJECTS

Title: Aaron Rebaza Selfie Semester/Year: Spring 2020 Software: Adobe Photoshop 2020
Description: The images seen here were used in the making of my final Photoshop Project. This look was inspired by the idea of the poster style art that has been seen throughout the internet. This poster style almost gives a sense of importance to whatever subject is placed in it.

Title: Aaron Rebaza Selfie Vector Semester/Year: Spring 2020 Software: Adobe Photoshop 2020
Description: This picture I used of myself was one I took in front of a small tile wall in Queens. The reason I used this picture was that I loved the pattern on the wall. In order to attain the final image results I had opened and scaled the jpeg image to fit my preset 1200px by 1200px. After doing this I used the color presets in image trace > make. Using the 16 colors preset made it pop and created more contrast. The shading on the hat was too dark and I went back and utilized the pen tool to create anchor points to fill in those areas with a lighter shade.
